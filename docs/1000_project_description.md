# ScholarHub – AI-Powered Student Productivity & Opportunity Platform

## Overview

ScholarHub is a web application designed to help students manage their academic journey from a single platform. Students often rely on multiple tools for study planning, doubt solving, tracking deadlines, and discovering opportunities such as internships, hackathons, and scholarships. This fragmentation makes it difficult to stay organized and make the most of available opportunities.

ScholarHub addresses this problem by providing a personalized dashboard where students can plan their studies, interact with an AI-powered academic assistant, track their progress, and discover relevant opportunities tailored to their interests.

---

## Problem Statement

Students face several challenges in managing their academic and professional growth:

* Academic tasks, deadlines, and study schedules are difficult to organize efficiently.
* Educational opportunities such as hackathons, internships, scholarships, and competitions are scattered across multiple websites.
* Students often lack a centralized record of their learning activities, questions, and progress.
* Switching between different applications for planning, learning, and opportunity discovery reduces productivity.

There is a need for a unified platform that combines academic assistance, productivity tools, and opportunity discovery into a single personalized experience.

---

## Proposed Solution

ScholarHub is an AI-powered student portal that enables users to:

* Create and manage personalized study plans.
* Interact with an AI assistant for academic support.
* Discover internships, hackathons, scholarships, and other opportunities.
* Save opportunities for future reference.
* Maintain a history of study plans, AI interactions, and saved opportunities.
* Monitor academic activities through a centralized dashboard.

The platform aims to improve student productivity, organization, and access to valuable opportunities.

---

## Key Features

### User Authentication

* Secure user registration and login.
* Personalized user profiles.
* Protected user data and activity history.

### Personalized Dashboard

* Overview of upcoming tasks and deadlines.
* Recent AI interactions.
* Saved opportunities.
* Study activity summary.

### AI Study Planner

* Generates customized study schedules based on subjects, deadlines, and available study time.
* Helps students prepare efficiently for exams and assignments.

### AI Academic Assistant

* Provides instant explanations for academic concepts and questions.
* Acts as a learning companion available on demand.

### Opportunity Discovery

Aggregates opportunities such as:

* Hackathons
* Internships
* Scholarships
* Competitions

Allows users to bookmark and track opportunities.

### Activity History

Stores:

* Previous study plans
* AI conversations
* Saved opportunities

Enables users to revisit past activities and monitor progress.
